About Us

The Office of Campus to Corporate facilitates recruitment, builds sustainable relationship with industries, and bestows resources equipped with professional skills for internships and fulltime employment. We bridge the transition of our students from campus to corporate individuals in a progressive manner. Effective training sessions are provided to secure lucrative jobs in top-notch organizations despite the market graph.
